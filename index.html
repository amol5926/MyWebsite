I am Alexander Mol

About me


I am Alexander Mol, a 19 year old student currently pursuing a Bachelor of science degree in Industrial Design Engineering Technology. From an early age, I showed an interest in science and mathematics, which led me to pursue my passion for technology. I’m an avid problem solver who loves working in projects that require creativity and ingenuity.

My diverse interests and passion for technology have led me to explore various fields, from engineering and design to photography and cinematography. With a strong work ethic and a deep desire to learn, my goal is to make an impact in the world of technology and design.

Portfolio

Flow

sep.-ongoing 2022-2023

Flow is a digital window that connects people with the outside world and helps to relax and restore energy during breaks from work or studying. It delivers an immersive experience that combines crisp audio and stunning visuals like intriguing miniature landscapes and natural displays to set the user’s mind at ease.

OYU

Feb.-June 2022

Buying appliances is pretty boring these days. Think about it, when was the last time you were excited to buy a kettle? OYU is a kettle designed for young adults that want something special. A square pixel matrix display or a mechanical iris in the lid, OYU has it all.

CyberLight

Sep.-Dec. 2021

CyberLight was developed as part of an assignment for the course introduction to industrial design. The main goal of the project, was to get familiar with the different elements of the design process. From sketching and quick and dirty prototyping, to making an actual product that meets the predefined design requirements.
